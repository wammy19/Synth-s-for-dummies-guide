Welcome.
This website is a guide to the different parts that make up a synthesiser, and how they are used to shape electronic sounds. The purpose of this guide is not to be a technical one, but to provide basic information with interactive examples of designing electronic sounds. This guide is intended for people with no prior knowledge but have a keen interest in synthesizers, because synthesizers are awesome. Click on 'Oscillator' to get going!
